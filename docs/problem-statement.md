# Problem Statement

## Project Title
International Student Housing Finder

## Background
International students often face major difficulties when trying to secure accommodation before or shortly after arriving in a new country. They may not know which suburbs are safe, affordable, or convenient for travel to campus. They may also struggle to identify trustworthy listings, compare rental options, and understand important information such as lease terms, transport access, and nearby services.

This creates stress at a critical stage of relocation and can negatively affect student wellbeing, budgeting, and academic readiness.

## Problem
There is no simple, student-focused platform that helps international students discover, compare, and shortlist suitable housing options based on their personal priorities such as budget, distance to campus, transport access, and safety.

Existing property platforms are often designed for the general rental market and do not address the specific needs of international students, including:
- limited local knowledge
- urgency of finding short-term or immediate accommodation
- difficulty verifying whether listings are suitable and trustworthy
- confusion around suburb selection, public transport, and essential amenities
- lack of a simple way to compare housing options in one place

## Proposed Solution
The proposed solution is a web-based platform called **International Student Housing Finder**.

The platform will help students:
- search available housing options near selected universities or campuses
- filter listings by price range, room type, lease length, and distance
- compare shortlisted options side by side
- view useful context such as estimated travel time, nearby supermarkets, and transport availability
- save preferred listings for future review

## Target Users
Primary users:
- international students planning to move for study
- newly arrived international students seeking accommodation quickly

Secondary users:
- education agents or student support staff helping students settle
- landlords or housing providers targeting student renters

## Project Goal
The goal of the project is to design and develop an initial solution that makes the housing search process easier, faster, and less stressful for international students.

## Value Proposition
This project provides value by:
- reducing the time students spend searching across multiple websites
- improving decision-making through clearer comparison of options
- helping students feel more confident about housing choices
- addressing a real and common pain point experienced by international students
